About This App
Purpose

A practical tool for vintage dealers to train pricing intuition and track market trends. Built around 9,000+ sold items from one of Japan's top vintage dealers.

About Mushroom Vintage

Mushroom Vintage has been operating out of Niigata, Japan since 2002. Owner Akira travels frequently to the USA to source inventory, and the shop is famous in Japan for providing the highest grade American vintage money can buy.

His pricing reflects what serious collectors actually pay for top-tier pieces — making this data invaluable for understanding the high-end market.

Features

📸 Gallery: Browse all sold items with search, sort by price or date.

🎮 Price Game: Test your pricing intuition. 20-round arcade mode or endless spaced-repetition study.

📈 Market Trends: Track what Akira is pushing — price movements and volume shifts by category, comparing this month vs trailing 6-month average.

💴 Currency Toggle: Train in USD or Yen to match your market.

Game Modes

20 Rounds: Quick arcade-style session. Guess prices, earn points, track your high score. Good for a fast warmup.

Endless Mode: Spaced repetition study. Items you miss come back more often. Tracks your accuracy per year range. This is where real learning happens.

Scoring (20-Round Mode)
  • Perfect (≤5% off): +500 pts
  • Great (≤15% off): +300 pts
  • Good (≤30% off): +150 pts
  • Close (≤50% off): +50 pts
  • Way off (≤100% off): -50 pts
  • Ouch (>100% off): -100 pts

Streak bonus: After 3 correct in a row, earn (streak - 2) × 25 bonus points.

Measurements

Key measurements are extracted from descriptions and displayed during the game:

  • Tops: Chest (body width) + Length
  • Bottoms: Waist + Inseam
  • Vests: Chest only

All measurements converted from cm to inches. Size matters for pricing — "golden size" (W32-34) and "big size" (44+) command premiums.

Categories Tracked

The Trends dashboard monitors 30+ categories based on vintage market segments:

Denim: Levi's (501XX, Big E, 506XX, 507XX, 557), Lee, Wrangler

Sweatshirts: Champion (Reverse Weave, Afterhoody), vintage brands

Military: Navy Deck, G-1, A-2, MA-1, M-65, P-44 HBT, Army Denim

Workwear: Premium brands (Carhartt, Sweet-Orr, Head Light), coveralls, chambray

Outdoor: Patagonia, L.L.Bean, North Face, Brown's Beach

Other: Souvenir jackets, Hawaiian shirts, band tees, Mackinaw

Data Source

All items scraped from vintage-mushroom.net. Only sold items ¥65,000+ (~$430 USD) included.

Prices converted at ¥150 = $1 USD. Descriptions auto-translated from Japanese.

Date range: 2015 - Present

Tech Stack
  • Frontend: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS
  • Backend: Python FastAPI
  • Database: PostgreSQL on Railway
